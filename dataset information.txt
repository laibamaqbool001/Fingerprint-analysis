Dataset: SOCOFing (Sokoto Coventry Fingerprint Dataset)

Source: Kaggle – SOCOFing (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ruizgara/socofing)

Images: Approximately 6,000 fingerprint samples

Classes: Three natural fingerprint pattern types — Arch, Loop, and Whorl

Alterations: The dataset also includes synthetically altered fingerprints (easy, medium, hard) to simulate real‑world tampering and test biometric robustness.
